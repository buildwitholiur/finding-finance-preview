Loans & Support

Personal Loans

Fair, affordable loans that put people first 

If a high street bank has turned you away or you need a small, manageable loan, a Community Development Finance Institution (CDFI) could help. CDFIs offer fair, affordable loans to people often excluded from mainstream banking. They take time to understand your situation and may also provide free support like budgeting advice or credit guidance.

What are you looking for a loan for?

What a personal loan from a CDFI covers

Personal loans from a CDFI are designed to be transparent, with no hidden fees, and often include free support like budgeting advice or credit-building guidance. They’re intended to help with important or unexpected costs, such as: 

  • Limited or poor credit history 

  • Irregular income

  • A need for a small loan amount

  • Life circumstances that don’t fit standard checks

You’ll always receive clear information about what you’re borrowing, with no hidden fees or confusing small print. 

How CDFIs assess personal loan applications

CDFIs look at your full financial picture, not just your credit score. This helps them understand what’s truly affordable for you. They may ask for: 

  • Details of your income and spending

  • Any benefits or grants you receive

  • Your plans for repaying the loan

A personal loan from a CDFI may take slightly longer to assess than one from a high street lender, as they take more time to review your overall financial situation.
