Social Enterprise Loans

Supporting social enterprises to build a better tomorrow

A Community Development Finance Institution (CDFI) exists to support organisations creating positive social or environmental impact, locally or across the UK. A CDFI will consider:

  • The “triple bottom line,” evaluating your social, environmental impact, as well as your financial position

  • Your need for flexibility, looking at tailored loans and grants that meet your needs products

  • Your unique business model

Who can apply for a loan from a social enterprise CDFI?

Social enterprises of all sizes and sectors can apply for a loan from a CDFI social enterprise lender. These lenders are especially suitable for social enterprises that:

  • Struggle to access finance from mainstream lenders

  • Have a mission to create social, environmental, or community impact

How does a CDFI business lender work?

CDFI business lenders take a personal, hands-on approach to your finance needs. A Relationship Manager will take time to understand your business and may consider wider benefits like job creation or investment in disadvantaged communities. They’ll guide you through the process, reviewing your financial position, growth plans, and overall potential, not just your annual accounts.
