Social Enterprise Loans

Supporting social enterprises to build a better tomorrow

A Community Development Finance Institution (CDFI) exists to support organisations creating positive social or environmental impact, locally or across the UK. A CDFI will consider:

  • The “triple bottom line,” evaluating your social, environmental impact, as well as your financial position

  • Your need for flexibility, looking at tailored loans and grants that meet your needs products

  • Your unique business model

Who can apply for a loan from a social enterprise CDFI?

Social enterprises of all sizes and sectors can apply for a loan from a CDFI social enterprise lender. These lenders are especially suitable for social enterprises that:

  • Struggle to access finance from mainstream lenders

  • Have a mission to create social, environmental, or community impact

What types of funding does a social enterprise CDFI offer?

Social enterprise CDFIs offer flexible, mission-aligned funding designed to help purpose-driven organisations grow and thrive. They typically provide a mix of the following: 

  • Loans with fair and affordable terms.

  • Grants to support specific projects or initiatives

  • Customised financial solutions to meet the unique needs of social enterprises

How will a social enterprise CDFI evaluate my loan application?

Unlike traditional lenders, CDFIs take a mission-driven approach to funding social enterprises. They look beyond the numbers to understand what your organisation does, why it matters, and its long-term financial viability. When assessing an application, they typically consider:

  • Loans with fair and affordable terms.

  • Grants to support specific projects or initiatives

  • Customised financial solutions to meet the unique needs of social enterprises

This approach enables CDFIs to support a wide range of purpose-led organisations, including those that may not qualify for mainstream finance.
