Loans & Support

Our CDFI members

All Finding Finance members are direct lenders committed to ethical practices, offering alternatives to high street loans and providing support for individuals, SMEs and social enterprises to get past restrictive lending rules by mainstream finance institutions.

Our lenders do this by going beyond traditional credit checks, using a more holistic approach to assess affordability and impact. Whether it is a personal loan for everyday expenses or unexpected bills, finance to grow a small or medium size business, or funding aligned with a social mission, our members offer responsible lending with a focus on long-term sustainability.

In addition to loans, many of our members provide extra support such as savings products, benefits checkers, and financial advice to help their lenders manage money better and access unclaimed grants or benefits.
