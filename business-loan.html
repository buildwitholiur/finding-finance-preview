Loans & Support

Business Loans

A CDFI provides a more personal, relationship-based way of lending, giving you access to the financial support you need to unlock your business’s growth

CDFIs can be a great option for small and medium-sized businesses (SMEs) that struggle to get finance from high street lenders. If you're a new business or have unique financial circumstances, a CDFI will look beyond the basics to assess your true potential. They offer loans to businesses of all sizes, sectors, and stages.

What a CDFI Business lender provides

CDFI lenders can provide loans from as little as £1,000 up to as much as £250,000, tailoring the support they provide to you in a fair and ethical way based upon your business’s unique circumstances.

How does a CDFI business lender work?

CDFI business lenders take a personal, hands-on approach to your finance needs. A Relationship Manager will take time to understand your business and may consider wider benefits like job creation or investment in disadvantaged communities. They’ll guide you through the process, reviewing your financial position, growth plans, and overall potential, not just your annual accounts.
