Community Development Finance Institutions

A Community Development Finance Institution (CDFI) provides alternative finance for underserved individuals, businesses, and social enterprises. Our CDFI membership empowers communities by offering tailored financial solutions to help them achieve their goals.

Our Impact

Finding Finance has been working at the forefront of supporting underserved individuals, businesses and social enterprises for over two decades. Our work is helping deliver real impact in disadvantaged communities across the UK, helping to shape a better future for society.

Discover more

About Us

As part of the UK’s industry body for Community Development Finance Institutions (CDFIs), Finding Finance helps underserved individuals, businesses, and social enterprises access alternative finance. Our free loan-matching service promotes fair finance and supports greater social impact.
