Our impact

Supporting individuals, SMEs, and social enterprises to build a brighter future

Our Community Development Finance Institution (CDFI) lenders help some of the UK’s most underserved people and organisations access the finance they need.

From supporting everyday financial needs to helping small businesses grow and partnering with social enterprises to drive social change, they are at the heart of inclusive finance.

More than lenders, our CDFIs invest in people, purpose, and the potential to build a better future.
